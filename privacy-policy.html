Privacy Policy

SkyLine Professional Logistics LLC values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you interact with our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, phone number, email address, and shipment details when you request a quote, contact our team, or use our services.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide vehicle shipping services, communicate with you about your shipment, respond to inquiries, and improve our services.

SMS Communication

By providing your phone number to SkyLine Professional Logistics LLC, you consent to receive SMS messages related to your vehicle shipping request or service updates. These messages may include shipment confirmations, pickup scheduling, driver details, delivery updates, and customer support responses.

Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. You may reply STOP at any time to opt out of SMS messages or reply HELP for assistance.

Information Sharing

SkyLine Professional Logistics LLC does not sell, rent, or share your personal information, including phone numbers, with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact SkyLine Professional Logistics LLC.
